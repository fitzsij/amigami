What is Amigami?

Our name "Amigami" comes from our lessons with the neighbourhood children who, being 5 years old couldn't pronounce Origami.

We sell unusual handmade origami jewellery in our beloved home in Cactus, a Japanese-specialist store located on the top floor of Stephen's Green Shopping Centre.
We also take custom orders, tailor-made just for you (just message us with your requirements, favoured design, colour, etc.)

We even offer Origami Lessons, if you would like to make some earrings of your own, or learn how to fold more complex designs.
Our ever-expanding list of earring designs include:

  • Butterflies
  • Dragonflies
  • Books
  • Sea Turtles
  • Dinosaurs
  • Lillies
  • Koi Fish
  • Rabbits
  • Ducks
  • Dolphins
  • Cranes

The standard price for our earrings is 20 euro (they take an hour minimum to make). Lessons are individually priced based on required materials, but are generally 10-15 euro an hour.
We aspire to create gifts for you and your loved ones that are worthy of being treasured.
